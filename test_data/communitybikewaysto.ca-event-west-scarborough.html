West Scarborough Rail Path (WSRP)

  • Sunday, February 22, 2026
  • 2:30 PM 4:30 PM
  • Albert Campbell Library 496 Birchmount Road Toronto, ON, M1K 1N8 Canada (map)
  • Google Calendar ICS

West Scarborough Rail Path (WSRP)

Community Bikeways is one of the featured speakers, and will present the Loop Trail initiative and talk about why it's an opportune time --- given Doug Ford's edict against new bike lanes that reduce car lanes --- to be connecting the strands of bike paths across the city that could become useful utilitarian routes. The Loop Trail and the WSRP are complementary initiatives.
