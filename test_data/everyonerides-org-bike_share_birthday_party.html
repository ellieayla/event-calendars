Bike Share Birthday Party

Come celebrate 11 years of Bike Share with us on Friday, March 20! We'll gather at the Workers Arts & Heritage Centre from 6:30-8:30 for family-friendly fun, food and giveaways 🎂🥳

If you want to start the party early, join us for our Birthday Group Ride! We'll roll out from the Sterling & Forsyth Bike Share Hub (AKA the McMaster Rainbow Crosswalk) at 6 PM and ride to the festivities together 🚲🎉
