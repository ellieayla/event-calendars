Tour De Cafe

Tour de Cafe is a novice cycling group ride for women/folks identifying as women. Each group ride incorporates knowledge about bikes and safe cycling with a ride to a local cafe. No bike? No problem. Contact NHCB as soon as possible! Have a bike, but it needs a tune-This up? Bring it to NHCB at least one week before our first ride to get it serviced. Gain confidence in cycling in the company of friends.

Meets every other Saturday at 8:00 am behind New Hope Community Bikes 1249 Main Street East, Hamilton

Tour de MTB

Interested in learning to mountain bike? Tour de MTB is a beginner women’s MTB ride that focusing on helping women develop basic mountain bike skills and gain confidence on the trails in the company of other women. This ride is beginner focused by more experienced riders are welcome to join!

Upcoming 2025 Tour de MTB ride dates:

  • July 23

  • August 13

Ride time and locations vary so check out the Facebook group for more information!
